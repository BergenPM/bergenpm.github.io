What we are

Bergen Perl Mongers is an organization founded with the purpose of building a local community around Perl in Bergen, Norway. We aim to do this by reaching out to companies who use Perl, and will try to engage students at the schools in the region.

Where we meet

As we have yet to have our first meetup, there is not much to say about this. Once we arrange the first event we will post information here.

You may reach out to @bergenpm on twitter or see if we are present in #bergen.pm on irc.perl.org.

Past meetups

This space will hold information on the previous meetups, once we've had some.

Sponsoring

To simplify management we rely on companies or individuals donating whatever things we need to operate. These things include rooms to hold sessions / meetings, food and drink for such events, swag, and also server-capacity.

Currently this webpage is hosted on github using Github Pages. The repository for this code can be found in the bergenpm.github.io respository under the BergenPM github account.

Who we are

This organization was formed by @jonisno and @christopherraa. If you have any questions just @-mention us or @bergenpm on twitter. If there is anything you would like to contribute to then let us know. The more people spreading a love for Perl the better.
